### India's Logistics Challenge [UPSC-M]

```
INDIA'S LOGISTICS PROBLEM — THE BIG PICTURE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CURRENT STATUS:
  Logistics cost in India: ~8.5–14% of GDP (declining)
  Global average: 8% of GDP
  USA: 7.5% | China: 10.5%
  Every 1% reduction in logistics cost = +0.5% GDP growth

WHY EXPENSIVE?
  1. Modal mix imbalance: 60%+ goods by road (most expensive)
     India: Road 60% | Rail 30% | Water 10%
     USA: Rail 40% | Road 35% | Water 15% (much more efficient)
  2. Poor infrastructure: bottlenecks at ports, checkposts
  3. Multiple clearances: GST improved but regulatory burden remains
  4. Cold chain gap: 37% of food wasted in transit (poor cold storage)
  5. Last mile: poor connectivity from villages to main roads

SOLUTIONS:
  → PM Gati Shakti: Integrated planning
  → Dedicated Freight Corridors: Faster + cheaper freight rail
  → Sagarmala: More goods by sea (cheapest mode)
  → National Logistics Policy 2022: Reduce cost to <8% by 2030
  → Multimodal Logistics Parks: Road + Rail + Warehouse integrated hubs
  → E-way bill under GST: Eliminated interstate checkposts
```

### Trade vs Development — UPSC Mains Framework

```
INDIA'S TRADE POLICY DILEMMAS [UPSC-M GS III]
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DILEMMA 1: FTA vs Domestic Industry Protection
  → More FTAs: Better export access; cheaper imports for consumers
  → But: Cheap Chinese/ASEAN goods kill Indian MSMEs
  → India has stayed away from RCEP (Regional Comprehensive Economic
    Partnership) — too much competition from China + ASEAN ★★★
  → Selective FTAs (UAE CEPA, Australia ECTA) instead

DILEMMA 2: Services Surplus vs Merchandise Deficit
  → India earns big in services (IT) but loses in goods
  → Needs to move up manufacturing value chain
  → China made this shift in 1990s; India must do it now
  → PLI scheme: Incentivise manufacturing of higher-value goods

DILEMMA 3: Oil Dependency vs Energy Transition
  → 85% of crude imported = permanent vulnerability
  → Every $10 rise in oil price = $15 billion extra import bill
  → Solution: Solar + Wind + EVs → reduce crude imports
  → India's renewable target: 500 GW by 2030 → trade balance improvement

DILEMMA 4: China Trade Imbalance
  → $85+ billion deficit with China = structural problem
  → Cannot decouple overnight (electronics, APIs, chemicals)
  → Strategy: China + 1 (attract companies leaving China → India)
  → PLI in electronics: Samsung, Apple suppliers now in India
```
